Tokens & compliance rules

Dynamic tokens: {City} and {State} are NewsBreak dynamic-content tags — the feed swaps in the reader's real location, which is the #1 native-CTR lever. Insert them via + Add dynamic content in the title field (don't type the braces literally).

Kept compliant: every headline sticks to Rugiet's real claims — 3-in-1 sublingual, ~15-minute onset, up to 36-hour duration, doctor-prescribed online, 81% prefer Rugiet. No fabricated FDA / cure / guaranteed-result claims (those get killed fast on NewsBreak's Rx review). 18+ / men's-health targeting.
